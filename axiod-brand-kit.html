Signal Blue — Primary Accent
Used for interactive elements, active states, data highlights, the logo mark, and primary CTAs. Never used for body text or decorative only purposes. Always ensure 4.5:1 contrast on dark surfaces.
Alert Orange — Critical States
Reserved exclusively for high-urgency threat notifications, critical incident badges, and destructive action confirmations. Overuse dilutes its meaning in an operational context — use sparingly.
Clearance Green — Safe / Resolved
Confirms system health, successful operations, cleared incidents, and online device status. Paired with Void background for maximum visibility on the tactical map and device panel.
Caution Amber — Warning States
Mid-tier threat level. Used for warning alerts, idle device states, and elevated risk indicators that require attention but are not immediately critical. Never use for primary actions.

Style Rules
Stroke-only, 1.2–1.8px weight. No fill except the sensor dot accent. Always use Signal Blue (#00D4FF) for icon color.
Grid System
All icons drawn on a 32×32 grid with 2px optical padding. Maintain consistent visual weight across the icon set.
Usage
Icons appear in nav, feature cards, and dashboard panels. Never resize below 16px. Provide 24px and 32px variants for different contexts.
05 — Brand Voice & Tone
Intelligent
Lead with insight, not jargon. Communicate the depth of the technology without overwhelming. Precision is credibility — every word earns its place.
Responsible
Acknowledge the human stakes. Security is not abstract — people's safety depends on the platform working. Honor that weight in every sentence written.
Mission-Driven
This isn't a product demo. It's a deployment briefing. Write as if the audience understands the cost of inaction in Zamfara or Bauchi — because they should.
Globally Competitive
Axiod competes with the best defense-tech companies on Earth. The copy, design, and data should make that undeniable to anyone who enters the room.
✓ Write Like This
Armed movement detected. Zone Zamfara NW-3. Response triggered. AI confidence: 91%.
Axiod has reduced incident response time by 63% in Zamfara since deployment — 18 months of field data, not projections.
The platform is built for the realities of low-bandwidth environments, not ideal lab conditions.
This is a security problem with a technical solution. Here's exactly how it works.
✕ Avoid This
Our revolutionary AI-powered solution is disrupting the security space with next-gen innovation!
We leverage synergistic robotics ecosystems to deliver value-added security outcomes for stakeholders.
Excited to announce our latest feature drop! 🚀🔥 #Startup #AI #Security
Our cutting-edge platform could potentially maybe help with some security challenges in certain regions.
