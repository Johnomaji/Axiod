Core Platform

Intelligence that works
where it's needed most

Six integrated modules deliver end-to-end security intelligence — from aerial reconnaissance to community-level risk communication.

Live Threat Radar
AI-processed sensor feeds deliver real-time threat mapping across all deployed zones, updated every 8 seconds. Pattern-recognition triggers precede human detection by minutes.
Autonomous Drone Fleet
Manage, route, and deploy surveillance drones via cloud. Geofencing, battery management, thermal imaging, and failsafe return protocols are fully automated.
AI Alert Engine
Natural language incident summaries generated by AI and routed to the right responders in under 15 seconds from detection — no analyst required in the loop.
Predictive Risk Modeling
Historical pattern analysis fused with environmental and movement data predicts high-risk windows 48–72 hours in advance, enabling preemptive resource deployment.
Community Alert Network
SMS, app, and radio-bridge alerts dispatched to community leaders, local responders, and registered civilians in real time — even in low-connectivity regions.
Unified Command Dashboard
A single-pane interface for security commanders to view all zones, manage all assets, and coordinate field response — from any device, anywhere in the world.
